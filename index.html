Geng Yang (杨埂)

Researcher @ Computing System Laboratory, Ant Research

I am currently a Researcher at the Computing System Laboratory, Ant Research (蚂蚁集团), working in the team led by Dr. Mingzhe Zhang. I received my Ph.D. degree in December 2024 from Xidian University, under the supervision of Prof. Yunsong Li and Prof. Jie Lei.

During my Ph.D., I was dedicated to Algorithm-Hardware Co-design for DNN architecture and high-performance FPGA acceleration. I developed efficient end-to-end DNN model inference systems (e.g., Stable Diffusion) by integrating hardware-friendly model pruning and quantization with FPGA-based hardware architectures.

Currently, I am diving deeper into computer architecture and expanding my research to Privacy-Preserving Computing, specifically exploring Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE)-based DNN acceleration.
